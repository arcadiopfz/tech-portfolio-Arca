AI for Business Decisions

The course "Artificial Intelligence for Business Decisions and Transformation" (CSCN8030), taught by Professor Glaucia Melo dos Santos, PhD, focused on AI's role in organizational decision-making. We, as students, explored Decision Support System (DSS) approaches, designed AI solutions, and worked in agile teams. The sprint cadence involved briefing, design, development, and demo stages. Ethical considerations and operational, tactical, and strategic decisions were discussed.
All the following projects were done by myself and my amazing group members, Hemasree Krishna Kumar and Jency Shijin ; we analyzed 6 types of businesses and prototyped AI solutions for them.
